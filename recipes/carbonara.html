Carbonara

Description

In this recipe we are creating an Italian dish call pasta Carbonara. The only ingrediants needed for this dish are listed below. This is a simple dish to make but hard to perfect.

Ingrediants

  • Pasta
  • Pancetta
  • Pasta water
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the 100g pancetta. Finely grate 100g pecorino cheese.
  3. Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl and mix with cheese
  4. Cook pasta as per package instructions
  5. While pasta is cooking, fry pancetta on medium heat until crispy and remove from heat
  6. When pasta is finished cooking reserve pasta water and add pasta to pan with pancetta
  7. Add ladel of pasta water to cheese and egg mixture
  8. Add mixture to pan with pasta and pancetta, using reserved water when needed and mix
  9. Serve and enjoy
